Creators Academy Siteplan

Site Name

Creators Academy

The name Creators Academy combines Creators reflecting the brand's mission to inspire individuals to build creative and innovative technological solutions to solve real world problems and Academy to teach and train in line with the brand's mission

Site Purpose

The website serves as platform for communicating the purpose, vision and mission of the brand, showcase upcoming trainings, academy curriculum, learning resources and contact information.

Scenarios/target audience possible questions

  • What trainings do you offer?
  • How much does a training cost?
  • How can I register as a student?
  • How long will it take for me to learn a skill?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #2f749e Steel Blue - Used for headings, call to actions and highlights
  • Secondary Color: #2b3033 - Used for text and backgrounds
  • Accent Color: #f5f5f5 - Used for text and backgrounds

Typography

  • Headings Font: Roboto Slab
  • Body Font: Roboto
